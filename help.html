Math:

Studying Tips:

- Try doing the problems on your own so you can work through them yourself.

- Review the slideshows/notes you get in class.

Resources:

- https://www.khanacademy.org/math

- https://www.ixl.com

Science:

Studying Tips:

- Participate in class discussions as they usually help you get a better understanding of the topic.

- Write down all the formulas you are given!

Resources:

- https://www.khanacademy.org/science

- https://www.pbs.org/wgbh/nova/


English/Language Arts:

Studying Tips:

- Read the material you're given and annotate parts that you need a better understanding of/are important parts of the book.

- Check that there's no plagiarizing in your papers and ask your friends to peer review them for you to double check.

Resources:

- https://www.sparknotes.com/

- https://www.smart-words.org/linking-words/transition-words.html


Social Studies:

Studying Tips:

- Look at the notes you get in class and try to apply some of those terms to real life.

- Do some of the readings on your own free time so you can participate more actively.

Resources:

- https://www.socialstudies.org/

- https://learninglab.si.edu/


Languages:

Studying Tips:

- Make sure to keep practicing the language you are learning so you are more able to have a good conversation in that language.

- If you don't understand a few phrases, look at different sentence structures and vocabulary terms.

Resources:

- https://www.alllanguageresources.com/

- https://translate.google.com/


Other:

Studying Tips:

- Ask for help in the subjects you're struggling with! If you don't understand, communicate that to someone who does.

- If you are having issues, ask for extra practice so you can work on fine tuning that skill.

Resources:

- https://www.khanacademy.org

- https://quizlet.com
